Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2026

The Valley Wholesale AZ LLC (“Valley Wholesale,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our website, create a wholesale account, place an order, communicate with us, or otherwise interact with our products and services.

By using our website or providing information to us, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

Information You Provide

You may provide information to us when you:

  • Apply for or create a wholesale account
  • Place or request an order
  • Submit a contact or credit application
  • Request product, pricing, shipping, or account information
  • Communicate with our sales, customer service, accounting, or compliance teams
  • Subscribe to email or text message communications
  • Participate in surveys, promotions, or other business programs

This information may include:

  • Your name
  • Business name
  • Job title
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • Billing and shipping addresses
  • Account username and password
  • Order and purchase history
  • Payment and billing information
  • Business licenses, resale certificates, tax-exemption documents, or other compliance records
  • Communications and customer-service records
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our website, we and our service providers may automatically collect information such as:

  • Internet Protocol address
  • Browser and device information
  • Operating system
  • Website pages viewed
  • Referring website
  • Date and time of access
  • Approximate location derived from an IP address
  • Cookie identifiers and similar technical information
  • Website interactions and usage activity

Information From Other Sources

We may receive information from third parties, including:

  • Sales representatives
  • Payment processors
  • Shipping and fulfillment providers
  • Customer relationship management platforms
  • Inventory and accounting systems
  • Business verification and compliance providers
  • Publicly available business records
  • Referral partners and other business contacts

2. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Create, review, approve, and administer wholesale accounts
  • Verify business, licensing, age, resale, tax, or regulatory eligibility
  • Process orders, payments, credits, returns, and refunds
  • Provide invoices, order confirmations, shipping information, and account notices
  • Communicate about products, availability, pricing, promotions, and services
  • Respond to questions and customer-service requests
  • Maintain customer, sales, accounting, inventory, and compliance records
  • Detect and prevent fraud, misuse, theft, security incidents, or unauthorized activity
  • Enforce our agreements, policies, and legal rights
  • Comply with tax, licensing, reporting, recordkeeping, and other legal obligations
  • Improve our website, products, services, and business operations
  • Perform analytics and understand how our website and services are used
  • Protect the rights, property, health, and safety of Valley Wholesale, our customers, and others
  • Carry out other purposes disclosed when information is collected or with your consent

3. Wholesale Account and Compliance Information

Certain products may only be purchased by qualified businesses or individuals who satisfy applicable age, licensing, tax, resale, or regulatory requirements.

We may request and retain documents needed to verify eligibility, including business licenses, resale certificates, tax documents, identification, shipping information, and other compliance records. We may decline, suspend, or close an account when required information cannot be verified.

4. Payments

Payments may be processed by banks, payment gateways, credit-card processors, or other financial service providers.

Payment providers may collect payment-card numbers, bank account information, billing addresses, and related transaction information under their own terms and privacy policies. We generally receive transaction confirmations and limited payment details rather than complete payment-card information.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We and our service providers may use cookies, pixels, tags, local storage, analytics tools, and similar technologies to:

  • Keep the website functioning
  • Maintain account sessions
  • Remember preferences
  • Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Measure website traffic and performance
  • Understand how visitors use the website
  • Improve products, services, and advertising

You may be able to control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may cause certain website features to function incorrectly.

Where required by law, we will provide additional choices regarding nonessential cookies or certain uses of personal information.

6. How We Disclose Information

We may disclose information to the following parties:

Service Providers

We may provide information to companies that perform services for us, including:

  • Website hosting and technology providers
  • Customer relationship management platforms
  • Payment processors
  • Accounting and invoicing providers
  • Inventory management providers
  • Shipping, freight, and fulfillment companies
  • Email and text messaging providers
  • Compliance and identity-verification providers
  • Analytics, cybersecurity, and fraud-prevention providers
  • Attorneys, accountants, insurers, and professional advisers

These providers may use information as necessary to perform services for us or as otherwise permitted by their agreements and applicable law.

Legal and Regulatory Disclosures

We may disclose information when we reasonably believe disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with a law, regulation, subpoena, court order, or legal process
  • Respond to a government, regulatory, tax, licensing, or law-enforcement request
  • Investigate fraud, security incidents, or illegal activity
  • Protect our legal rights and enforce our agreements
  • Protect the safety or property of Valley Wholesale, our customers, or others

Business Transactions

Information may be disclosed or transferred in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, sale of assets, bankruptcy, or other business transaction.

With Your Direction or Consent

We may disclose information for other purposes when you direct us to do so, consent to the disclosure, or the disclosure is otherwise described when the information is collected.

7. Sale and Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell personal information in exchange for money.

Some website analytics, advertising, or cookie activities may be considered a “sale,” “sharing,” or use for targeted advertising under certain state privacy laws, even when no money is exchanged. Where applicable, we will provide legally required notices and methods for exercising opt-out rights.

We do not sell or share mobile phone numbers or text-message opt-in information with third parties for their independent marketing or promotional purposes.

8. Email Communications

We may send transactional or administrative emails concerning:

  • Account activity
  • Orders and payments
  • Invoices and statements
  • Shipping and delivery
  • Compliance requirements
  • Changes to our services or policies
  • Customer-service communications

We may also send promotional emails regarding products, inventory, pricing, or other business opportunities. You may unsubscribe from promotional emails by using the unsubscribe link in the email or contacting us.

Unsubscribing from promotional emails will not prevent us from sending necessary transactional, account, compliance, or legal communications.

9. Text Messages

When you provide a mobile telephone number and consent to receive text messages, we may send messages regarding:

  • Product and inventory updates
  • Order placement and processing
  • Payment or account questions
  • Shipping and tracking information
  • Customer-service communications
  • Other business-related matters

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. You may reply HELP for assistance.

Mobile information and text-message opt-in information will not be sold or shared with third parties for their independent marketing or promotional purposes. We may disclose this information to messaging platforms, telecommunications carriers, and service providers as necessary to deliver and administer the messaging program.

Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing products or services unless otherwise clearly disclosed.

10. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Maintain your account and provide services
  • Complete transactions
  • Satisfy tax, accounting, licensing, compliance, and recordkeeping requirements
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements
  • Prevent fraud
  • Protect our legal rights

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information, the nature of our relationship, and applicable legal requirements.

11. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no website, network, storage system, or electronic transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee that information will never be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed without authorization.

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for notifying us promptly if you believe your account has been compromised.

12. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you reside and whether a particular privacy law applies to Valley Wholesale, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we maintain about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of certain personal information
  • Obtain a portable copy of certain information
  • Opt out of certain sales, sharing, targeted advertising, or profiling
  • Limit certain uses of sensitive personal information
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Appeal the denial of a privacy request
  • Receive equal service and pricing without unlawful discrimination for exercising a privacy right

These rights may be subject to exceptions. For example, we may retain information needed to complete transactions, maintain business and tax records, comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, or establish and defend legal claims.

To submit a privacy request, contact us using the information in the “Contact Us” section below. We may need to verify your identity and authority before completing a request.

Where permitted by law, an authorized agent may submit a request on your behalf. We may require proof that the agent is authorized to act for you.

13. Third-Party Websites and Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, applications, payment platforms, shipping services, or social media pages.

We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy, security, or content practices of third parties. You should review their privacy policies before providing information to them.

14. Children and Age-Restricted Products

Our website and services are intended for business customers and are not directed to children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our age-restricted products are not offered for purchase by anyone who does not satisfy applicable minimum-age and eligibility requirements.

If we learn that we collected personal information from a child in violation of applicable law, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

15. International Visitors

Our website and services are operated from the United States. Information provided to us may be processed and stored in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

Privacy and data-protection laws in those locations may differ from the laws in your jurisdiction.

16. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, services, technology, or legal obligations.

When we update the policy, we will revise the effective date at the top. Material changes may also be communicated through the website, by email, or through another appropriate method.

Your continued use of our website or services after an updated policy becomes effective constitutes acknowledgment of the revised policy.
